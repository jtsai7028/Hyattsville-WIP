Most Severe/Extreme Flooding Areas

Due to the flooding from the Anacostia River through Hyattsville, we felt that it would be beneficial to research areas most susceptible to flooding. After looking through multiple databases, we were able to pinpoint locations within Hyattsville categorized as “severe-flood risk areas”, which we have marked on the map. Although other areas were categorized as a “moderate/less severe flood risk areas”, we decided to only show the locations that were marked as the most vulnerable to reduce clutter.
